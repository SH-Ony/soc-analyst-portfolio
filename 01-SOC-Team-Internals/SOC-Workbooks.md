
# SOC Workbooks and Playbooks

## What is a Playbook?

A playbook is a predefined step-by-step procedure for handling a specific type of security incident.

Examples include:

- Phishing
- Malware
- Brute Force
- Ransomware

---

## Benefits

- Consistency
- Faster investigations
- Reduced human error
- Easier analyst onboarding
- Improved compliance

---

## Typical Playbook

1. Receive alert
2. Validate alert
3. Gather evidence
4. Investigate indicators
5. Determine severity
6. Contain incident
7. Escalate if necessary
8. Document findings
