
# SOC Metrics and Objectives

SOC metrics help measure the effectiveness and efficiency of security operations.

---

## Common Metrics

### Mean Time To Detect (MTTD)

Average time required to identify an incident.

Lower is better.

---

### Mean Time To Respond (MTTR)

Average time required to respond to an incident.

Lower is better.

---

### False Positive Rate

Percentage of alerts that are not genuine threats.

Lower false positives improve analyst productivity.

---

### Alert Volume

Total number of alerts processed during a specific period.

---

### Escalation Rate

Percentage of alerts requiring escalation to higher-tier analysts.

---

## Why Metrics Matter

Metrics help organizations:

- Improve detection
- Optimize workflows
- Reduce response time
- Measure analyst performance
- Improve security posture
