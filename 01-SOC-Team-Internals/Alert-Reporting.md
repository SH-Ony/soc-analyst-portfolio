
# SOC Alert Reporting

## Purpose

Accurate reporting ensures that security incidents are communicated clearly to senior analysts, incident responders, and customers.

A good report allows another analyst to understand the incident without repeating the investigation.

---

## Report Structure

- Alert Summary
- Affected Assets
- Timeline
- Indicators of Compromise (IOCs)
- Investigation Performed
- Findings
- Risk Assessment
- Recommended Actions

---

## Escalation Criteria

Incidents should be escalated when:

- Malware is confirmed
- Sensitive systems are affected
- Multiple hosts are compromised
- Privilege escalation is observed
- Data exfiltration is suspected

---

## Key Lesson

Good documentation is essential. An incomplete investigation report can delay incident response even if the technical analysis is correct.
