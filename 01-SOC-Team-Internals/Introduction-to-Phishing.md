
# Introduction to Phishing

## Overview

Phishing is a social engineering attack that attempts to deceive users into revealing sensitive information or executing malicious content.

SOC analysts are often responsible for performing the initial investigation of reported phishing emails.

---

## Investigation Process

1. Examine sender information
2. Verify email headers
3. Inspect URLs
4. Check attachments
5. Identify Indicators of Compromise (IOCs)
6. Determine risk level
7. Escalate if malicious

---

## Common Indicators

- Spoofed sender address
- Urgent language
- Suspicious hyperlinks
- Unexpected attachments
- Credential harvesting pages
